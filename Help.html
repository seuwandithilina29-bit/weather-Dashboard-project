Help & Support

How to use the Weather Dashboard

  • Enter a city name in the search box
  • Click the Search button
  • View current weather and details
  • Use Settings to change units or theme

Frequently Asked Questions

Why is my city not found?
Make sure the city name is spelled correctly and try again.
Why is the temperature wrong?
Check your temperature unit (Celsius/Fahrenheit) in Settings.
App not loading weather data?
Check your internet connection or API key configuration.
